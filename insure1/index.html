How It Works

Simple steps for landlords to protect their rental properties

1

Dial USSD Code

Landlords dial *347*10# from any mobile phone to access the insurance menu and learn about available coverage options.

2

Sign Up & Link Policy

Complete registration using your mobile number and link your rental properties to your insurance policy.

3

File Claims via SMS

When incidents occur, send a structured SMS like "CLAIM FIRE 12345" to initiate the claims process immediately.

4

Track & Receive Payouts

Receive real-time updates on your claim status and get payouts directly to your mobile money account.
